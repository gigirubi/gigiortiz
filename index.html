About Me

I am at my happiest when I am being creative.
This is why I am a UX Designer. I use creativity to solve puzzles and the idea of creating a unique solution excites me. Designing beautiful, functional, inspirational designs is my goal. My life as a kid was filled with odd artsy hobbies such as making mud pies, dressing up as a clock for a play, and turning my homework into origami. I wasn’t the first pick for P.E, but I was never the last one standing for a group project. I enjoy making references from The Office, playing videogames with my friends, and asking my dog why he is so cute. You can find my designs on Dribble and my blog on Medium.
